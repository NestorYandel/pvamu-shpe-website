Who We Are

The Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers at Prairie View A&M University is dedicated to empowering Hispanic students in STEM fields through professional development, academic support, and community engagement.

Our chapter provides a supportive network where students can develop leadership skills, connect with industry professionals, and make lasting impacts in their communities and careers.

We are committed to increasing Hispanic representation in engineering and STEM, fostering excellence, and creating opportunities for our members to thrive academically and professionally.

Through workshops, networking events, mentorship programs, and community service, we prepare our members to become leaders in their fields and advocates for diversity in STEM.
