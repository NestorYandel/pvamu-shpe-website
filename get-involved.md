      <div class="gi-section-header">
        <span class="section-label">HOW TO JOIN</span>
        <h2 id="gi-steps-title" class="gi-section-heading">Become a Member in 5 Steps</h2>
        <p class="gi-section-sub">Joining is straightforward — follow these steps and you'll be part of the familia in no time.</p>
      </div>
      <ol class="gi-steps-list" aria-label="Membership steps">

        <li class="gi-step" data-animate>
          <div class="gi-step-number" aria-hidden="true">1</div>
          <div class="gi-step-content">
            <h3 class="gi-step-title">Attend a General Body Meeting</h3>
            <p class="gi-step-desc">Come to one of our general body meetings to meet the chapter, hear about upcoming events, and get a feel for what SHPE PVAMU is all about. No commitment required — just show up and say hello.</p>
          </div>
        </li>

        <li class="gi-step" data-animate style="--delay: 0.07s">
          <div class="gi-step-number" aria-hidden="true">2</div>
          <div class="gi-step-content">
            <h3 class="gi-step-title">Create a Free SHPEConnect Account</h3>
            <p class="gi-step-desc">Head to <a class="gi-link" href="https://www.shpeconnect.org/eweb/DynamicPage.aspx?WebCode=LoginRequired&expires=yes&Site=shpe" target="_blank" rel="noopener">SHPEConnect</a> and create your free account. This is SHPE's official member portal and your gateway to scholarships, the Career Center, and all national resources.</p>
          </div>
        </li>

        <li class="gi-step" data-animate style="--delay: 0.14s">
          <div class="gi-step-number" aria-hidden="true">3</div>
          <div class="gi-step-content">
            <h3 class="gi-step-title">Purchase Your National Membership</h3>
            <p class="gi-step-desc">Once logged in, click <strong>"Join/Renew Membership"</strong> and follow these steps: select <strong>Region 5</strong> as your region, then select <strong>Prairie View A&amp;M University</strong> as your chapter. Fill out all required account and educational information, agree to the Code of Ethics, then add the membership to your cart and complete payment. Make sure to <strong>take a screenshot of your payment confirmation</strong> — you'll need it for chapter registration.</p>
          </div>
        </li>

        <li class="gi-step" data-animate style="--delay: 0.21s">
          <div class="gi-step-number" aria-hidden="true">4</div>
          <div class="gi-step-content">
            <h3 class="gi-step-title">Fill Out the Chapter Registration Form</h3>
            <p class="gi-step-desc">Complete the SHPE PVAMU chapter registration form so we can add you to our official roster and keep you informed about events, opportunities, and updates throughout the semester.</p>
            <button class="btn btn-gold gi-step-btn" type="button" disabled>
              Chapter Form — Link Coming Soon
            </button>
          </div>
        </li>

        <li class="gi-step" data-animate style="--delay: 0.28s">
          <div class="gi-step-number" aria-hidden="true">5</div>
          <div class="gi-step-content">
            <h3 class="gi-step-title">Start Attending Events &amp; Earning Points</h3>
            <p class="gi-step-desc">You're officially in! Show up, get involved, and start building your member points. The more active you are, the more opportunities open up — including a chapter-sponsored trip to the SHPE National Convention.</p>
          </div>
        </li>

      </ol>

        <span class="section-label">FAQs</span>
        <h2 id="gi-faq-title" class="gi-section-heading">Frequently Asked Questions</h2>
      </div>
      <div class="gi-faq-list">

        <details class="gi-faq-item">
          <summary class="gi-faq-question">Is SHPE only for Hispanic students?</summary>
          <div class="gi-faq-answer">
            <p>No — SHPE is open to everyone. While SHPE was founded to uplift and empower Hispanic students in STEM, the organization welcomes all students who believe in that mission. All backgrounds, majors, and identities are welcome at SHPE PVAMU.</p>
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="gi-faq-item">
          <summary class="gi-faq-question">Does my major matter?</summary>
          <div class="gi-faq-answer">
            <p>Not at all. While our focus is on STEM fields, students from any major are welcome to join. If you have an interest in science, technology, engineering, or math — or simply want to be part of a driven, supportive community — there is a place for you here.</p>
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="gi-faq-item">
          <summary class="gi-faq-question">How much does membership cost?</summary>
          <div class="gi-faq-answer">
            <p>SHPE national membership for undergraduate students is <strong>$15 per year</strong>. This gives you access to national scholarships, the SHPE Career Center, discounted convention registration, and much more. Chapter registration (Step 4) is completely free.</p>
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="gi-faq-item">
          <summary class="gi-faq-question">What if I cannot afford the membership fee?</summary>
          <div class="gi-faq-answer">
            <p>We understand that every dollar matters. Reach out to the chapter officers — we may be able to assist with fee support or direct you to available financial resources. We never want cost to be a barrier to joining the familia. Email us at <a class="gi-link" href="mailto:shpe.pvamu@gmail.com">shpe.pvamu@gmail.com</a>.</p>
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="gi-faq-item">
          <summary class="gi-faq-question">When does membership renew?</summary>
          <div class="gi-faq-answer">
            <p>SHPE national membership renews annually, typically at the start of each academic year. You can renew through your SHPEConnect account at <a class="gi-link" href="https://shpe.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener">shpe.org</a>. We will send reminders to all chapter members when renewal season approaches.</p>
          </div>
        </details>
